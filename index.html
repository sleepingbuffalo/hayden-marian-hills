The Course

A links course in the middle of Montana

Designed by John Steidel, Marian Hills rolls across 90 acres of northern prairie with vast fairways, elevation changes, and greens averaging 8,000 square feet. Four sets of tees keep it fun for beginners and honest for low handicappers — and everyone's welcome, it's a public course.
